In this episode of the Enigmatic Baboon I review the two-part finale of Doctor Who series 8 "Dark Water" and "Death in Heaven". The intriguing "Dark Water" featuring the big reveal about Missy, Danny's sad fate and Clara's conflict with the Doctor.

The emotional and beautiful with "Death in Heaven" the crazy plan of an old enemy, many returns and the Doctor taking charge more than he would like.

Episode 8: Drawing Like Rob Liefeld

Rob Liefeld is one of the most controversial yet successful comic book artists. Many pundits say that he does not deserve his success because supposedly he cannot draw. I quite like Rob Liefeld's artwork.

Episode 7: HobbitCon 2014

The HobbitCon 2014 in April in the German city of Bonn was an amazing get-together of fans, experts, celebrities and artists to celebrate the works of J.R.R. Tolkien.

In this episode of "The Enigmatic Baboon" I visit the HobbitCon and experiences fun, friendship, fandom and fantasy. See the amazing workshops about masks and prosthetics, drawing elves, stunt fighting, building armor and film make-up. See writer and director Tommy Krappweis spill the beans on his new fantasy movie "Mara und der Feuerbringer" and how the special effects for the dragon (Lindwurm) were made. See the actors playing the dwarves in "The Hobbit" tell intimate stories about embarrassments in the past. And see how the participants of the costume competition got the whole auditorium to join in singing "Misty Mountain Cold" while waiting for an indecisive jury to choose the best costume.

Episode 4: InterComic 2013

The Intercomic 2013 is a small but interesting comic convention in Cologne, Germany. On November 2nd, 2013 I went there to meet comic book artists, read comic books and be surprised by the diligence of some makers. Illustrator Diana Kennedy had written, drawn and produced her own animated feature. Comic book artist Andreas Eikenroth continued drawing his comic book among the commotion. All this and more: Toys, books, original art, posters and comics!

Episode 3: Drawing A Dragon

In this episode we take a look at drawing a fire breathing dragon using pencil, pen and computer coloring with Pixelmator on a Mac.

I first sketch out the dragon on paper and then ink it. After scanning it, I clean the image up in Pixelmator and then color it digitally.

Episode 1: The Fantastic Mr. Byrne (Fantastic Four Review)

In the very first episode of "Enigmatic Baboon" I rediscover the comic book artist John Byrne and take a look at "Fantastic Four" No. 232. This is the first issue of Byrne's epic run on the series that went on for more than 60 issues.

The story is called "Back to Basics" because Byrne takes the Fantastic Four back to their original qualities and characterization as defined by their creators Jack Kirby and Stan Lee.
